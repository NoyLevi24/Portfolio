About Me

As a former combat soldier in the IDF Search and Rescue Brigade, I've transitioned into a dynamic and results-driven DevOps Engineer, bringing the same discipline and problem-solving skills from my military service to the world of technology. Right after completing my military service, I got into the Combat Soldiers to High-Tech Program, where I completed an intensive one-year DevOps course.

My journey continued with a three-month intensive internship at Octopus Computer Solutions, where I gained hands-on experience in building robust, scalable, and efficient infrastructure. I've developed expertise in core DevOps with a strong focus on automation and cloud technologies.

My technical toolkit includes Kubernetes, Terraform (IaC), Helm, ArgoCD (GitOps), and comprehensive observability tools like Prometheus and Grafana. I've successfully engineered and deployed cloud-native platforms using microservices architecture on AWS, establishing full GitOps workflows for automated multi-environment continuous deployment. I'm certified as an AWS Solutions Architect, Terraform Associate, RHCSA, RHCE, and CCNA, with strong foundations in networking, Python, and Linux.
